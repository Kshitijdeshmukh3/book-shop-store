About Mechanical Engineering

The Mechanical Engineering (MECH) department is dedicated to the design, development, and manufacturing of machines and mechanical systems. With a strong foundation in thermodynamics, materials science, fluid mechanics, and robotics, MECH engineers play a key role in almost every industry.
